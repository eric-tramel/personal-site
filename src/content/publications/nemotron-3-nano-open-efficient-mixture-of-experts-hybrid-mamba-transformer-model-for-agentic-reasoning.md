abstract: >-
  Nemotron 3 Nano 30B-A3B is a Mixture-of-Experts hybrid Mamba-Transformer
  language model pretrained on 25 trillion tokens and then post-trained with
  supervised fine-tuning and large-scale reinforcement learning. The model
  improves over the prior Nemotron 2 Nano generation while activating fewer
  parameters per forward pass, with strong throughput, reasoning, chat, and
  agentic performance and support for contexts up to 1M tokens.
